ANSELM V4 — Free Browser Strategy Game, No Download Required

ANSELM is a free single-file HTML5 browser game. One wrapping cylindrical world with five organic biome zones: Pink Lowlands (SW, starting area), Green Forest (NW), Purple Highlands (N, Nexus Eye source), Blue Coast (NE), and Beige Desert (SE). The world wraps east to west — travelling far enough east returns you to where you started. Play as the Traveller guiding native civilisations from Age 0 (Primitive) through Age 10 (The On and On). A single Nexus Eye corruption source in the Purple Highlands spreads outward as the world develops. Forge five biome weapons and the Ultimate Weapon to defeat the Eye. Free, single HTML file, no install, works offline.

Five Biomes

Pink Lowlands (SW, player start): Ashfawn animal, cured meat and salt resources, Ashen Blade biome weapon, gateway always open.

Green Forest (NW): Mosscrawler animal, herbs, resin, lumber resources, Verdant Spear biome weapon, gateway requires Ashen Blade.

Purple Highlands (N, Nexus Eye at approx. tile 88,22): Voidmoth animal, ore and rare crystals, Voidreaver biome weapon, gateway requires Verdant Spear.

Blue Coast (NE): Driftray animal, fish, pearls, dyes resources, Tidecaller biome weapon, gateway requires Voidreaver.

Beige Desert (SE): Dustleaper animal, spice, glass, dates resources, Duskbrand biome weapon, gateway requires Tidecaller.

Native Civilisation

Each biome supports native civilisations that build, gather, trade, mate, and dispute autonomously. Towns progress through ages 0-10. A 10-trait genetic inheritance engine governs native DNA: 50/50 parent blending with 15% mutation rate. Hard caps: 80 living natives per biome, 150 corrupted natives total, 4 towns per biome.

Lux in Tenebris

Ten-chapter philosophical text driving civilisation ages 5-10. The characters: Mind (computation), Body (construction), Soul (operator within), Self (unified current-state), Order (spiral of everything), Life (perceived value and existence), Time (phases and becoming), Eternity (the eternal on and on), Death (end of phase and rebirth), Repeat (the program begins again).

The Nexus Eye

The Nexus Eye sits in the Purple Highlands at approximately tile coordinate (88,22). It functions as an active faction, spreading goo corruption outward as the world develops. Corrupted natives spawn from goo-covered tiles. The Eye is destroyed using the Ultimate Weapon forged from all five biome weapons.
